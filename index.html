Overview

What is Microsoft SharePoint?

🏛️

Your organization's digital home base

SharePoint is your central hub for storing, organizing, and sharing documents and information across your team. Build intranet sites, manage files, and collaborate in real time — from anywhere.

📂 Document Libraries 🌐 Intranet Sites 🤝 Co-Authoring 🔒 Permissions
5 Steps to Get Started
1
🌐

Access SharePoint

Go to sharepoint.com or open it from the Microsoft 365 app launcher (the waffle icon). Sign in with your @nebraska.gov account.

💡 Pin it to your browser for quick access
2
🏠

Find Your Sites

Your SharePoint Home shows sites you follow, recent activity, and recommended content. Look for your department or team site.

💡 Click ★ to follow a site and find it faster
3
📂

Open a Document Library

Inside your site, click Documents in the left nav. Browse folders, upload files, or create new ones directly in the browser.

💡 Drag & drop files to upload instantly
4
✏️

Edit & Co-Author

Click any Word, Excel, or PowerPoint file to open it in your browser. Multiple people can edit at the same time — no more emailing versions!

💡 Changes save automatically — no Ctrl+S needed
5
🔗

Share a File or Folder

Right-click any file and choose Share. Send a link by name or email, and choose whether they can edit or only view.

💡 Set an expiry date on shared links for security

Features

Key Features to Know


🌐

Team Sites

Dedicated spaces for each team or project with shared documents, news, and calendars.

📰

News & Pages

Publish announcements, how-to guides, and rich content pages to keep your team informed.

📂

Document Libraries

Store files with version history, check-in/check-out, and metadata tagging for easy retrieval.

🔒

Permissions & Sharing

Control exactly who sees what with granular read, edit, and owner-level permissions.

🔍

Powerful Search

Find files, people, and content across all your sites instantly with intelligent search.

📱

Mobile App

Access sites, files, and news on the go with the SharePoint mobile app for iOS and Android.

Pro Tips for Beginners
📌

Pin Your Favorite Sites

On SharePoint Home, hover over a site and click the star icon to follow it and keep it at the top of your list.

🕒

Use Version History

Right-click any file → Version History to see all past saves and restore an earlier version if needed.

🔔

Set Alerts on Files

Right-click a file or folder and choose Alert Me to get an email whenever someone makes changes.

🗂️

Use Metadata, Not Folders

Tag files with metadata columns (Department, Status, Year) so you can filter without deep folder structures.
